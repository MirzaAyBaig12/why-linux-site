Finding a Better Fit

For years, I used Windows because it was what came with most computers. Eventually, I started looking for alternatives and discovered Linux. What started as curiosity turned into a complete switch.

This website is about my experience with Linux, why I prefer it, the distribution I use every day, and how you can get started if you're interested in trying it yourself.

Linux isn't perfect, and it isn't for everyone. But for me, it offered more control, more customization, and a better overall experience. This site explains why.

Why I Left Windows

I used Windows for years because it came pre-installed on most computers I had access to.

It worked fine for everyday tasks, but over time I started noticing limitations.

Things felt locked down, updates were sometimes annoying or forced, and customization was limited compared to what I wanted from a system.

That’s what eventually pushed me to explore Linux.
